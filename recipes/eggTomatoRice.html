Egg Tomato Rice

Fried rice with egg and Tomato

Freshly cooked rice stir fried with an egg and some cherry tomatoes, a delicious meal with some sweet and sour from the tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Cook rice (Rice cooker or pot)
  2. Hunk of butter in a pan on medium
  3. Add rice
  4. Stir until hot
  5. Throw in egg (don't throw it too hard)
  6. Cook until egg is almost done
  7. Add tomatoes and bury them (want them to sweat and wrinkle)
  8. Serve
